About Us.

Hey there, welcome to Group 12's site! We're taking Computer Science 2212, the Introduction to Software Engineering course at Western University. The main part of the course is to build a software project.

We're working on a web application to help people discover restaurants in their area. We've found that it's hard to find new restaurants to try. Even if there are a couple on your list, it's hard to coordinate your friends to head over together. That's why we're introducing three features: a world map to help you discover international food options located nearby, a trending restaurants page, and a voting system to get friends together.
